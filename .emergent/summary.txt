To resolve this, the AI engineer identified the discrepancy by comparing the structure of the new Defence exams with a correctly working exam (like SSC). The fix involved modifying  to ensure that for NDA, Agniveer, CDS, and UPSC CAPF AC, each subject (e.g., Mathematics, General Awareness) now correctly contains a topics key, under which the actual topic names and their details are listed.
