<product_requirements>
The Ceibaa platform aims to be a dynamic, multiplayer competitive exam preparation platform for Indian students. It leverages Google Sheets for question data across 8 major exam categories (NEET, JEE, UPSC, SSC, Banking, Agriculture, RPSC, Defence), each with a detailed syllabus and topic-wise quizzes. Key quiz modes include Solo Practice (random 10 questions, no timer), Live Battle (1v1 real-time matchmaking, WebRTC, live leaderboards, scoring with streaks), and PIN-based Room Quizzes. User features include login, performance tracking, analytics, leaderboards, rewards, and social elements like gifts, reactions, and chat. A Creator Dashboard for educators to manage rooms and content is also planned. The implementation has progressed through core quiz, live battle, social features, and partial UI redesigns. The immediate focus is on refining the logo and ensuring core battle functionalities are robust. Google Sheets integration for question banks is crucial.
