<product_requirements>
The Ceibaa platform is a web-based competitive exam preparation tool with two main user-facing features: Solo Practice and real-time Live Battles. A central requirement is that all question content is sourced dynamically from Google Sheets.

The platform must include a social layer that allows users to follow each other, view a mixed timeline of posts from followed users and trending content, and share battle room codes. This social functionality must be integrated with the core quiz experience, allowing users to follow others from a battle room and see battle results posted to the feed.

A secure, role-based Admin Panel is required for platform management. This panel must provide a real-time overview of platform statistics (users, posts, battles), a detailed user management table, and a crucial Exam Sheet Manager. The Sheet Manager is the interface for connecting Google Sheets to the system, mapping them to specific exams or classes, and importing the questions into the database. The import process must be robust, handling specific sheet formats and providing clear feedback to the admin. Authentication includes both demo accounts and social logins (Google, Facebook, Twitter), with a seamless flow that maintains the user's session across the application.
</product_requirements>
